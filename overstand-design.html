Current Product

Automation Foundations Course

The Automation Foundations Course teaches how to turn a repetitive business process into a simple automated workflow using Google Forms, AI, Google Sheets, and automated email responses.

The course uses Henry's Pressure Washing Business as a practical example to demonstrate how customer information can be collected, processed, stored, and used to generate email responses.

This course is available for direct purchase. Other services listed below require contact before purchase.
