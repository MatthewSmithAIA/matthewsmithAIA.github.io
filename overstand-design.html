# Overstand Design ## Practical AI Automation Training & Workflow Optimization Overstand Design is an independent initiative focused on helping individuals and small businesses leverage artificial intelligence, workflow automation, and process improvement techniques to increase productivity, identify opportunities, and solve real-world business challenges. Our goal is to make practical AI and automation accessible through training, educational resources, and hands-on workflow examples that can be applied across a variety of industries. ## Products and Services ### AI Automation Training Digital training materials designed to help users understand and implement practical automation workflows using commonly available business tools and AI technologies. Topics may include: * Workflow automation fundamentals * Business process improvement * Google Workspace automation * AI-assisted productivity systems * No-code and low-code automation concepts * Prompt engineering fundamentals ### ATS Resume Optimization AI-assisted resume analysis and optimization services designed to help job seekers improve applicant tracking system (ATS) compatibility, identify missing keywords, strengthen positioning, and better align their experience with target roles. Deliverables may include: * ATS compatibility analysis * Resume optimization recommendations * Missing keyword identification * Recruiter-style feedback * Career path recommendations * Interview preparation guidance ## Delivery All products are delivered digitally.
