Bridging Tech Expertise to New Hires

Business Problem

Manufacturing facilities often rely on experienced technicians who develop workarounds and operational knowledge that never make it into manuals. When these employees retire or leave, that knowledge disappears, creating downtime, onboarding challenges, and safety risks.

Solution

Developed an AI-powered knowledge retention workflow using UiPath. The system captures technician expertise through interviews, logs, or text-to-speech, extracts key information using AI, and stores approved knowledge in a searchable repository for future use.

Workflow

The workflow captures technician responses, extracts structured data, classifies severity, and routes information based on risk level. Low and medium severity issues are stored automatically while critical issues are escalated to management for review.

Tools Used

  • UiPath
  • Google Sheets
  • JSON
  • Gmail
  • RPA
  • AI extraction

Business Impact

  • Preserves expert knowledge
  • Accelerates onboarding
  • Reduces downtime
  • Improves operational consistency
  • Supports management oversight
  • Reduces knowledge loss risk
